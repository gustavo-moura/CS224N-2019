Context-Free Grammar (CFG) Rules and Abbreviations

* CFG rules are different for each language

NP - Noun Phrase    NP -> Det N  NP -> Det (Adj) N
PP - Prepositional Phrase
VP - Verb Phrase

N - Noun
NN - singular Noun
NNS - plural Noun
Adj - Adjective
P, Prep - Preposition
Det - Determiner
V - Verb
num - numerical modifier
amod - adjective modifier
